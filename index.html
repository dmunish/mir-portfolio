Services

Architectural Visualization

We create photorealistic renderings that bring unbuilt projects to life. From intimate residential spaces to large-scale urban developments, our images communicate design intent with clarity and emotional impact.

Animation & Film

Moving imagery that tells the story of a space. We craft cinematic sequences that guide viewers through environments, revealing how light, materials, and human activity shape architectural experience.

Landscape Visualization

Specialized rendering of outdoor environments, parks, and urban landscapes. We capture the interaction between architecture and nature, seasonal changes, and atmospheric conditions with exceptional realism.

About

We are a bunch of normal people with a not so normal job: To create enthusiasm!

We generate enthusiasm by crafting beautiful images of architecture and landscapes. Our goal is to make images that help people see clearly what an amazing world we live in.

Enthusiasm is powerful because it is the first spark of change.

Profile

Mir was founded by Mats and Trond in the year 2000, when they were students at the Norwegian Academy of Art and Design. To this day, they are still best friends and partners, sharing the responsibilities as principals at Mir.

The Mir team consists of around 10 handpicked and ambitious artists from all over the world. Our goal is to not just be a service provider, but a source of inspiration for the architects and their projects.

Working at Mir

Mir is always on the lookout for new team members. We read through all the applications we receive, but are unfortunately not able to respond to everyone.

If you're qualified, we will write back. If you don't receive a reply, don't hesitate to apply again. In fact, we've hired people on their fourth application...
